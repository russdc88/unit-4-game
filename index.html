Crystal Collector!

Instructions

At the beginning of the game you will be given a random number.

There are four crystals below. When you click a crystal, you will be given amount of points. Each time you click a crystal, you score will add up.

To win you must get your points to equal the random number. If you go over, you lose.

You will not know the value of the crystal until you click it.

After the game is over, the game will reset with new numbers for the crystal and a new random number.
